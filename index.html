    WHY CHOOSE US

    We aim to revolutionize the adopton of sustainable energy solutions by offering plug and play containerized solar systems to commercial clients through a lease-to-own model. We provide a cost-effective and eco-frindly alternative to traditional energy sources, enabling businesses to harness clean energy while minimizing initial investment barriers. We empower businesses to transition to sustainable energy practices by providing accessible and efficient plug and play containerized solar systems.

    HOW WE WORK

    Energy Audit

    We review energy bills and meter your facility in order to determine the energy profile and demand.

    System Design & Review

    Our team of engineers will design a custom solar system for your business that will be cost effective and with considerable savings. Financing options will also be available for review.

    Contract Negotiation & Signing

    Once we have reached a satisfactory response with you on the details of the installation, financing plan, operation and maintenance details, both parties will sign a contract and installation will commence shortly.

    Installation & Commissioning

    Our team of expert solar installers will deliver a plug and play high quality installation with Tier 1 equipment. The system will be configured and commissioned and you will start saving money while reducing the imapact of your operations.

    BENEFITS OF WORKING WITH US

    Reduced Energy Bill

    Better energy savings compared to grid or diesel generators.

    Environmental Benefit

    Powering your business off solar eliminates tons of greenhouse gasses that pollute the earth making your operation cleaner and eco-friendly.

    Financing

    We help you lessen the financial burden so you can focus on your business operations.

    PPA or Lease to Own

    As you pay for your electricity, you also pay for your solar system and eventually get to own it.
